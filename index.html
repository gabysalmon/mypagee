About gaby

I'm Gaby Salamanca, a high school sophomore. I've had some painful experiences with love that have affected my view on romantic relationships. These experiences have made it difficult for me to love myself and believe in the possibility of having a healthy, lasting relationship. I often struggle with my self-esteem and my expectations about love. Despite these challenges, I have a deep empathy for others. I make an effort to understand and share the feelings of those around me, and I always try to be the best version of myself. I find great comfort and support in my family, who I love dearly. Additionally, my cat, Lola, is a special source of unconditional affection. Her presence provides me with a constant source of love that helps me through tough times. In summary, even though I face difficulties in the realm of love and struggle with self-acceptance, my empathy and the love I have for my family and my cat Lola form the foundation of my emotional well-being and hope for the future..
